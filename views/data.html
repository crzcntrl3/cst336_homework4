What kind of data is collected?

The types of data collected are complex consisting of:

DNA Sequences
- Full human genomes
Amino acid sequences
- Protein sequences
Metabolomics
- The nature and traffic patterns of transformations of small molecules by the biochemical pathways active in cells
Transcriptomics
- The pattern of RNA synthesis from DNA
Proteomics
- Distribution of proteins in cells
