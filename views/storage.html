The short answer to this is the data is collected and stored by large data banks located all over the world. Various data banks collect DNA and RNA sequences from scientific papers and genome projects. Most data banks belong to international entites to allow for a more collaborative environment. Retrieval of this information from the archieves utilizes very standard tools such as Google. One simple need to search specific keywords, and that information is accessed instantaously.
